About Andrew Eugene

Andrew Eugene is a singer-songwriter hailing from the rugged shores of Cape Breton. With heartfelt lyrics and melodies inspired by the landscapes and stories of his home, Andrew's music weaves together folk, roots, and a touch of East Coast soul. His latest album, 1,000,000 Miles, is a journey through love, longing, and the roads that connect us all. Whether performing in cozy venues or writing by the sea, Andrew brings authenticity and passion to every note.
